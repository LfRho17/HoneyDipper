# HoneyDipper
Computational solution to Laplace's Equation $ \nabla^{2} V = 0 $ on non symmetrical geometry. Finding the electric field $ \mathbf{E} = -\nabla V $ . We could started by the method of relaxation. 

## Motivations:
In electrodynamics, the analytical solution to Laplace's equation is limited to certain configurations, although in real life these solutions fall somewhat short.
